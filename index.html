5-Day Dumbbell Workout

Link to Workout

Day 1: Chest, Shoulders & Triceps

  • Dumbbell Bench Press (5 sets, 8-10 reps)
  • Incline Dumbbell Bench Press (4 sets, 8-10 reps)
  • Dumbbell Floor Press (3 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Standing Dumbbell Press (4 sets, 8-10 reps)
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raise (3 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Tricep Kickbacks (3 sets, 8-12 reps)

Day 2: Legs & Core

  • Dumbbell Goblet Squat (4 sets, 8-10 reps)
  • Dumbbell Stiff Leg Deadlift (4 sets, 8-10 reps)
  • Dumbbell Rear Lunge (4 sets, 8-10 reps each)
  • Dumbbell Frog Squat (3 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Calf Raise (4 sets, 20 reps)
  • Weighted Crunch (3 sets, 20 reps)
  • Side Planks (3 sets, 20 secs each)

Day 3: Back & Biceps

  • Dumbbell Bent Over Row (4 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Tripod Dumbbell Row (4 sets, 8-12 reps each)
  • Dumbbell Pullover (3 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Reverse Grip Dumbbell Row (4 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curl (3 sets, 10-15 reps)
  • Dumbbell Hammer Curl (3 sets, 10-15 reps)

Day 4: Legs & Core

  • Dumbbell Squat (4 sets, 8-10 reps)
  • Dumbbell Deadlift (4 sets, 8-10 reps)
  • Dumbbell Split Squat (3 sets, 8-12 reps each)
  • Dumbbell Hip Thrust (4 sets, 10-15 reps)
  • Dumbbell Calf Raise (4 sets, 20 reps)
  • Dumbbell Side Bends (3 sets, 15 reps each)
  • Plank (3 sets, 20 secs)

Day 5: Complete Upper Body

  • One Arm Dumbbell Rows (4 sets, 8-10 reps each)
  • Dumbbell Arnold Press (4 sets, 8-10 reps)
  • Incline Dumbbell Bench Press (4 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Chest Supported Dumbbell Row (3 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Pinwheel Curl (2 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extension (3 sets, 8-12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Shrug (3 sets, 12-15 reps)
